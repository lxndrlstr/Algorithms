How to Make Creamed Corn

Ingredients:

  • 2 pounds corn kernels
  • 2 cups unsweetened almond milk (divided)
  • 1 teaspoon sea salt
  • 2 tablespoons granulated white sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • Pinch of freshly grated nutmeg
  • 1/4 cup freshly grated parmesan cheese

Instructions:

  1. In a skillet over medium heat, combine the corn, 1 cup of almond milk, salt, sugar, pepper, and butter.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the remaining cup of almond milk and flour. Add the slurry to the corn.
  3. Continue to cook, stirring occasionally, until mixture is thickened, about 10 minutes. Taste for salt, adding a little more if necessary. Add the pinch of nutmeg along with the parmesan cheese, stir to combine and simmer until melted.
  4. To serve, top with a pinch of flaky sea salt and freshly ground black pepper.
